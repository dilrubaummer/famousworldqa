Our Services

Electrical Services

A wide range of electrical services, including design, installation, and maintenance of electrical systems, such as distribution board, power, and control systems, as well as troubleshooting and repairs.

ELV Works

Design, installation, and maintenance of extra-low voltage systems, such as security, fire alarm, PA SYSTEM, CCTV BMS, ACCESS DOOR CONTROL, PLC SCADA, TELEPHONE, AV systems and communications systems.

Solar Systems

Offering both on-grid and off-grid solar solutions for homes, schools, and businesses. Our systems are individually sized to suit user needs and can be implemented in remote areas or scattered business entities.

Building Maintenance

Comprehensive electrical maintenance services including inspection, testing, and maintaining electrical systems in the workplace. We ensure the safety and reliability of wiring, circuit breakers, lighting, outlets, and electrical appliances.
